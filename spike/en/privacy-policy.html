Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes the data practices of Jouni Miettunen (the "Developer", "we", or "us") regarding the mobile application Spike (the "Game").

Data Disclosure: The Game is an offline-only application. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information to the Developer or any third parties.

1. Data Collection and Usage

The Developer does not collect any personal information from users.

  • Offline Operation: The Game functions entirely locally on your device and does not require an internet connection for core gameplay.
  • Personal Information: We do not request or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Authentication: No account creation or registration is required to access the Game.

2. Tracking and Analytics

No tracking mechanisms are integrated into the Game.

  • Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services or SDKs.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We do not access or use device advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) for tracking behavior across apps.

3. Crash Reporting

Technical logs and crash data are not transmitted to the Developer. Any logs generated during a crash remain on the user's device and are handled by the operating system according to your device settings.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience. Because the Game collects no data from any users, it is inherently compliant with privacy regulations regarding children, as no personally identifiable information is processed.
